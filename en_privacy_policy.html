Welcome to Defanzo. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy aims to clarify our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

1. Application Overview

Defanzo is an application designed to meet your digital media processing needs. The application provides a range of services including:

  • Image and video compression
  • Image resizing
  • Image format conversion
  • Video trimming

Our goal is to provide an effective and user-friendly tool to enhance and manage your media files while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to improve the stability and performance of the application. This service collects:

  • Information about errors and crashes that may occur during app usage
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version used

The purpose of collecting this data is to enable us to quickly identify and fix issues, ensuring you have a smooth and error-free experience.

2.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our application. We collect anonymous data about:

  • Number of app opens
  • Most used features
  • General usage patterns

This information helps us make informed decisions to improve the app and develop new features that better meet your needs.

2.3 Firebase Messenger

We use Firebase Messenger to send important notifications to you. These notifications may include:

  • New app updates
  • Announcements about new features
  • Important information related to app security and performance

Our aim is to keep you informed about the latest developments and ensure you have the best possible experience.

2.4 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. Through this service:

  • AdMob may collect information about your device and your interaction with ads
  • This information is used to provide ads more relevant to your interests
  • We do not share any personal information with advertisers

We adhere to Google AdMob's privacy policies and work to ensure your privacy is not violated.

3. Required Permissions

3.1 Storage Access

We request permission to access your device's storage for the following reasons:

  • To read images and videos you want to process
  • To save processed files (compressed, converted, trimmed) on your device

Without this permission, the app cannot perform its core functions. We assure you that we do not access any files other than those you choose for processing.

3.2 Notification Permission

We request permission to send notifications to enhance your app experience:

  • To inform you about the progress of long processing operations such as compressing large files
  • To alert you about important app updates
  • To send tips to improve your use of the app

You can always disable notifications from your device settings if you prefer not to receive them.

4. Protecting Your Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy to the fullest extent possible. To achieve this:

  • As we do not collect personally identifiable data, we have no individual data to provide to you.
  • We do not require account creation or login to use the app
  • The data we collect is technical and non-personal, therefore it cannot be modified or corrected.

5. Your Rights and Choices

We respect your right to control your data. You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of any data related to you (considering we do not collect personal data)
  • If you wish to stop sharing crash data, you can do so by uninstalling the app or discontinuing its use.

6. Compliance with Laws and Data Protection

We respect our users' privacy and are committed to complying with applicable laws, including the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable:

  • Limited Data Collection: We only collect anonymous crash data to improve app performance. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • Use of Firebase Analytics and AdMob: These services may collect anonymous data about app usage to improve performance and display relevant ads.
  • User Rights: We respect our users' privacy rights. If you have any queries or concerns about your data, please contact us.
  • Security: We take appropriate security measures to protect the data we collect from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. In case of significant changes:

  • We will notify you through an in-app notification
  • We will request your consent to the new terms before you continue using the app
  • We will provide the ability to review the previous version of the policy
